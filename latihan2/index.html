About Us

What is ZTech

ZTech is a new business that was only established in June 2015. ZTech is a tech business focused on the millennial age and its fascination with technology.
Our vision is to create a top technology education platform that inspires young people to pursue careers in technology.
We have a mission to accelerate Indonesia's transition to the digital world via education and technology that transforms people's lives.

A new business called ZTech wants to create a developer environment in Indonesia. ZTech, which was established on June 10th, 2015, offers a number of services that might assist young people in advancing the field of technology.
